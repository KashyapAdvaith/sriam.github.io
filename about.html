Our Doctor

Dr. Nagendra Babu

Dr. P.Nagendra Babu was a graduate in Bachelor of Ayurvedic Medicine and Surgery (BAMS) from Ragiv Gandhi University of Health Sciences (RGUHS), Bangalore. After his graduation he was associated for a 4 years clinical experience with renowed Ayurvedic Physician Dr Dhenuvakonda Sreerama Murthy, SRIAM hospital, Hyderabad. Dr. Nagendra underwent intensive training sessions held at Rajasthan, Chennai and gained extensive knowledge regarding integrating ancient marma therapy in regular pain management practice. Dr. Nagendra is a pioneer in resolving spine problems such as sciatica, cervical spondylitis, lumbar disc bulge and chronic skin disorders. Dr. Nagendra is best known for his counselling in various psychosomatic disorders and male impotency. Dr. Nagendra gained rich expertise in dealing with infertility cases with outstanding positive results. Dr Nagendra is successfully in management of obesity, sleep apnea and diabetes with aid of diet modifications, yoga and physical exercises
